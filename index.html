FAQ

How does it work ?

Open the spotify app and scan it by clicking on the photo icon next to the search bar, your song should magically start playing.

How are the keychains made ?

They are modelded with OnShape and then the plastic part is 3d printed using PLA.

What about shipping ?

I ship them anywhere in France using postal services. Every order is tracked. Yes, I can ship internationally if you give me a large amount of money.

Are there any other colors available ?

Yes ! I currently have green, pink, red and orange. I can print in any color if you give me a little bit more money.
